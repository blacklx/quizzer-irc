# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

The Quizzer IRC Bot collects the following information:

### Automatically Collected:
- **IRC Nicknames**: Your IRC nickname when you participate in quizzes
- **Quiz Scores**: Your scores from quiz games
- **Game Participation**: Records of quiz participation and answers

### Not Collected:
- Personal identifying information (real names, email addresses, etc.)
- IP addresses (handled by IRC server, not the Bot)
- Private messages (only processed for commands, not stored)
- Location data

## 2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely for:

- **Leaderboard Display**: Showing top scorers and rankings
- **Game Functionality**: Tracking scores and game progress
- **Bot Operations**: Ensuring proper quiz game operation

## 3. Data Storage

- **Location**: Data is stored locally in SQLite database files
- **Retention**: Scores and participation data are retained indefinitely
- **Access**: Data is accessible only to Bot administrators

## 4. Data Sharing

- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share your data with third parties
- **Public Leaderboards**: Quiz scores may be displayed publicly via `!leaderboard` command
- **Administrative Access**: Bot administrators may access data for maintenance purposes

## 5. Question Data

- Questions are sourced from Open Trivia Database (opentdb.com)
- Question content is publicly available and not considered private
- No personal data is associated with questions

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **View Your Data**: Use `!leaderboard` to see your scores
- **Request Deletion**: Contact Bot administrator to request score deletion
- **Opt Out**: Simply stop using the Bot

## 7. Security

- Data is stored in local database files
- No encryption is applied to stored data
- Access is limited to Bot administrators
- Standard IRC security practices apply

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the Bot.

## 9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## 10. Contact

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests, contact the Bot administrator.

## 11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- General data protection principles
- IRC network privacy standards
- Open-source software best practices
